Web Application Projects

TECHTOK

A tech stock info site for beginners.

Stock trading has been proven to gain popularity among the public, especially investment in technology sector. However, fierce competition between various stock apps leads to increasingly complicated interface with tremendous technical details, which turns out to be user-unfriendly for newcomers.

This project aims to create a simple yet comprehensive information site of tech sector stocks for zero-experience investors, in order to help the inexperienced to grasp the newest move of tech equities with quotes and news, and build up their own watchlists.

Features: Market information/My Fave/Personal Blog

Main Role: Frontend layout/Fave function(Backend)/Tag function(Backend)/API calls

TRIPAL

A platform for making new trip pals.

When it comes to the travel apps nowadays, they serve users with comprehensive travel information. For us, an unforgettable trip should not only be the product of wonderful sightseeing, but also the acquaintance of new faces during the voyage.

The goal of our proud TRIPAL is to let users visit new places in the company with new mates, and experience the delightness of new face-to-face connections.

Graphic Design Projects

(Client: SE Production Limited)

Company Logo

SE Production Limited is a new-founded publishing and educational company that provides exercises and online tutorial services for senior secondary students.

I use shamrock as the overall impression of the logo, with the outline that shows the lower characters of the company title and Möbiusband, or commonly known as the mathematical sign for infinity.

The cute leaf represents the developing adolescent, as well as the messages behind: courage and wisdom, which were often praised in Western poems. Besides, the exclamation symbol on the top-right corner interprets discovery to the solution, new ideas and understanding towards knowledge.
