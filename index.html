About Me

I was born, raised and educated in Shatin District, Hong Kong. After completed my bachelor's programme of Economics in the Chinese University of Hong Kong, out of the others' expectations, I work as a student tutor of the juniors.

Having served for two years, I found myself not being satisfied as a tutor only. there might be more stuff to be achieved. I slowed myself down to consider my future thoroughly, and started to explore my potential.

Apart from starting my freelance work in designing book covers, I got myself a refreshing odyssey to Japan, in order to seek direction for my bottleneck period.

The Turning Point

I have once thought of being a programmer before, but the thought was put aside due to the conception that only the graduates of computer science-related programmes are favoured.

During my lost period, my curiosity towards the industry was aroused, and discovered programmers are desired everywhere, due to the ultra-speed development of technological sector around the globe. More important, many of them do NOT have any academic background of Computer Science. They are all well-trained programmers from distinct sectors.

'Perhaps here's the opportunity?' And finally, I found Xccelerate.

My Little Thought

Learning coding skills of various languages is definitely my goal (and it looks cool!).

I am excited to get myself fully immersed into this technology realm, as well as the industry, through this programme.
